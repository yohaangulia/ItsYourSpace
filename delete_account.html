Request Account Deletion - YourSpace

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from the YourSpace app, please send a request to:

Email: itsyourspaceapp@gmail.com

What will be deleted?

  • Your account (email, first name, last name)
  • All associated personal information

What will be retained?

No data will be retained after your account is deleted.

How long will deletion take?

All deletion requests are processed within 7 business days.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us via the email above.
